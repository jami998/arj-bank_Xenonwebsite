Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and solutions

How do I qualify for a business line of credit?

Qualification for a business line of credit typically requires at least 6 months in business, a minimum credit score of 600, and annual revenue of $100,000 or more. Our application process is streamlined to provide quick decisions.

What security measures do you have in place?

We employ bank-level encryption, multi-factor authentication, biometric verification, and continuous monitoring to ensure your financial data remains secure. Our security systems are regularly audited and updated.

Can I integrate Xenon with my accounting software?

Yes, Xenon Bank integrates seamlessly with popular accounting software including QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and more. This allows for automatic syncing of transactions and simplified financial management.

What makes Xenon different from traditional banks?

Unlike traditional banks, Xenon offers faster approval processes, more flexible lending criteria, lower fees, and innovative digital tools designed specifically for modern businesses. Our customer-centric approach means personalized solutions for your unique needs.

Is there a minimum deposit requirement?

No, there is no minimum deposit requirement to open a Xenon business account. We believe in making banking accessible to businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises.
